Visit a Meeting

Interested families and prospective scouts are always welcome to visit our weekly meetings. No appointment necessary.

When to Visit

Thursdays at 7:30 PM

Who to Bring

Scout + parent or guardian

We encourage prospective scouts to bring a parent or guardian so everyone can learn about the troop.

Menlo Church

950 Santa Cruz Ave. Enter through the main entrance and follow the signs. Parking is in the main lot.
