About Troop 566

Over 50 years of building character, leadership, and lifelong friendships

Our Mission

Troop 566 has been part of the Menlo Church community since 1973. Our mission is to prepare young people to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling in them the values of the Scout Oath and Law.

We believe that the best way to grow is through experience — in the wilderness, in the community, and among peers who challenge and support you. Every scout in our troop is given real responsibility and the chance to lead.

Whether it's your first campout or your Eagle Scout project, Troop 566 is here to help you grow into the person you want to become.

Our History

1973

Troop Founded

Troop 566 was chartered at Menlo Church in Menlo Park, welcoming its first cohort of scouts and beginning a tradition of outdoor adventure and service.

1985

First High-Adventure Expedition

The troop completed its first multi-day backpacking trip in the Sierra Nevada, setting the tone for decades of wilderness exploration.

2000

25th Eagle Scout

Troop 566 celebrated its 25th Eagle Scout — a milestone that reflected the troop's deep commitment to mentorship and advancement.

2023

50 Years Strong

The troop celebrated its 50th anniversary with alumni returning from across the country to camp, reconnect, and honor a half-century of Scouting.
