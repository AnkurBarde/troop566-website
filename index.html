Who We Are

Troop 566 has been serving our community since 1973, giving young people the chance to build leadership skills, explore the outdoors, and make lasting friendships.

Our troop focuses on outdoor adventure, community service, and personal growth. We learn by doing, and our scouts take on real responsibilities that prepare them for life.

What We Do

From high-adventure trips to community service, we offer activities that challenge and inspire our scouts.

Outdoor Adventures

Monthly camping trips, backpacking expeditions, and high-adventure activities that build confidence and outdoor skills.

Leadership Development

Youth-led programs where scouts plan, organize, and lead activities, building essential life skills.

Community Service

Regular service projects that make a difference in our community and teach the value of giving back.
